# Web2Comics Short Demo Video Script

Duration target: 2:30 to 3:00  
Format: screen capture + light zooms + captions  
Audience: new users evaluating the extension quickly

## 1. Hook (0:00-0:15)
Screen capture:
- Open a news page (CNN or Wikipedia article).
- Show extension icon click.
- Show `Create Comic` button.

Voiceover:
- "Turn any page into a visual comic summary in seconds."
- "Web2Comics automatically finds the best story and turns it into panels."

On-screen text:
- "From webpage to comic in under 15s"

## 2. Two-click flow (0:15-0:40)
Screen capture:
- Click `Create Comic`.
- Keep defaults visible: 3 panels, fast provider.
- Click `Generate Comic`.
- Show progress briefly, then first panel appears.

Voiceover:
- "Default flow is intentionally simple: Create Comic, then Generate."
- "The extension auto-picks a top story and starts rendering immediately."

On-screen text:
- "2 clicks: Create Comic -> Generate"

## 3. Story auto-selection + manual override (0:40-1:05)
Screen capture:
- Show `Grounding` indicator.
- Open `Stories` button and list of detected stories with summaries.
- Select a different story and re-generate.

Voiceover:
- "Web2Comics detects multiple story candidates and auto-selects the strongest one."
- "You can switch to any other detected story when you want control."

On-screen text:
- "Auto-pick + manual story selection"

## 4. Generate from highlighted text (1:05-1:25)
Screen capture:
- Highlight a paragraph on the page.
- Open extension.
- Switch source mode to `Use highlighted text`.
- Generate.

Voiceover:
- "Need precision? Highlight specific text and generate from exactly that content."

On-screen text:
- "Generate from selected text"

## 5. Viewer capabilities (1:25-1:55)
Screen capture:
- Open side panel viewer.
- Switch layout presets.
- Show panel controls: regenerate panel/caption, factual/simpler actions.
- Expand compact grounding/facts section.

Voiceover:
- "After generation, use viewer tools to refine panel quality and layout."
- "You can regenerate per panel instead of rerunning the full comic."

On-screen text:
- "Per-panel edits + multiple layout presets"

## 6. History and reuse (1:55-2:15)
Screen capture:
- Open history mode.
- Show thumbnail cards.
- Open a prior comic.

Voiceover:
- "Every comic is saved in history for quick reopen and reuse."

On-screen text:
- "History with visual previews"

## 7. Share and export (2:15-2:40)
Screen capture:
- Open share menu.
- Show targets (X, LinkedIn, Story, Email).
- Trigger `Download`.
- Briefly show exported image with attribution line.

Voiceover:
- "Export or share instantly to social channels and email."
- "Each output includes source-aware branding for attribution."

On-screen text:
- "Share-ready exports"

## 8. Closing CTA (2:40-3:00)
Screen capture:
- Back to webpage + generated comic side by side.
- Show extension name and icon.

Voiceover:
- "Web2Comics helps you summarize, learn, and communicate faster."
- "Install the extension and create your first comic now."

On-screen text:
- "Web2Comics - From content to comic"

---

## Quick Recording Checklist
- Use one CNN page and one Wikipedia page.
- Record at 1440x900 or 1920x1080.
- Keep cursor movements slow and intentional.
- Add chapter title cards between sections (1 second each).
- Use subtitles for all voiceover lines.
- Keep total under 3 minutes.

## Optional B-roll Inserts
- Story picker close-up.
- Grounding indicator close-up (green/yellow/red).
- Before/after panel regenerate.
- Share menu open animation.
