Quick Recording Checklist

  • Use one CNN page and one Wikipedia page.
  • Record at 1440x900 or 1920x1080.
  • Keep cursor movements slow and intentional.
  • Add chapter title cards between sections (1 second each).

  • Keep total under 3 minutes.
